Gameplay Event: Two-player Puzzle

Event Details: The amusement park opens the “Two-Player Puzzle” event. Directors must cooperate, splitting tasks to activate switches at the same time and ignite fireworks to clear levels!

Gameplay Description: During the event period, directors can team up with another director to solve puzzles. There are a total of 7 levels to complete. Completing challenges will reward Bright Iron and exclusive event avatars.

Event Duration- 12/8/24 - 12/29/24

Dou Di Zhu (Card Game)

Gameplay Description: The event follows the traditional rules of “Dou Di Zhu.” Each player is dealt 17 cards after a round of bidding to determine the landlord. Two farmers team up against the landlord. The player who plays all their cards first wins for their team.

Gameplay Location: Located on the right side of the Lion District Flower Market plaza. Interact with the Dou Di Zhu table to start playing.

Event Duration - Begins with the launch of version 1.2 on 12/6/24.

Event: Rise of the Fishing Winds

Gameplay Description: As winter arrives, the streets of Lion District are filled with anticipation for fresh seafood. Team up with the seafood stall owner to go fishing! You can earn Bright Coins, event-exclusive avatars, and other leveling materials.

Event Duration - 12/27/24 - 1/17/25

Event: Phantom Dream Reflection

Gameplay Description: During the event, complete tasks to earn event items (“Cat Bell Wishes”) that can be exchanged for the Uncommon costume “Phantom Dreamlight” and in-game items.
