DEVELOPER MESSAGE

Current events and news from Guilongchao
BY Kafka (text), Gema (translation), December 2024

DUAL-PERSON PUZZLE EVENT

The director on the left, the assistant on the right! Work together with trust and synergy to solve the mysteries! The “Dual-Person Puzzle” event is about to begin. Participate to unlock dual-person avatars and win generous rewards!

Event Time - 2024/12/8 10:00 – 2024/12/29 03:59

Event Details

- After the event starts, directors can invite friends or recruit teammates to form a duo team to play. Directors can also solo and be matched with other players to complete 7 puzzles together.

- During the event, the 7 puzzles will be unlocked sequentially over time. Directors must complete the previous puzzle to unlock the next one.

- In each puzzle, the directors will control “Team Personas” to solve mysteries. Both players must reach the final point to complete the puzzle. If you encounter difficulties, pay attention to the puzzle mechanism and communicate through quick messages or voice chat with your teammate.

- Each puzzle offers a one-time reward upon first completion.

Note! Completing the main task “The Joyful Legend” unlocks access to the event.

Event Rewards - Gacha currency, Avatar, Coins
