Version 1.2 Update and Maintenance Notice

Dear Director:

The new version of "Fabricate False Fantasy " will officially begin on December 6! The following is the updated content of this version.

Update and compensation

Update time: 2024/12/06 08:00 ~ 2024/12/06 11:00(gmt+8). (The actual update time may be advanced or delayed depending on the actual release situation)

Update method: Restart the game to complete the update.

Note! We will temporarily close the server at 2024/12/06 08:00(gmt+8), and all directors will not be able to log in during the maintenance. To ensure that your account data is normal, please close the game in advance before maintenance. Thank you for your understanding and support.

Compensation Details

Compensation: Ming Pearl(pull currency)* 300 + box of time sand(60 stamina) * 1

Eligible Recipients: Director with reputation level "assistant one" before 2024/12/06 08:00(gmt +8)

The compensation will be issued via in-game mail within 9 hours after the game update.

New Content


New Quest - Classic · Crossing Wangchuan*

Ferrying others to ferrying yourself. Longchao Pavilion has received an unexpected commission from a psychopomp ...

Availability: Permanent after the version update.

Eligible conditions: Complete Main Story Quest "Set off for White Lion Street" and reach director reputation level "Trumpcard 3".

Notes from Alkane: *忘川 wangchuan - one of the many significant river from Chinese mytho underworld. Literal translation: the river of to forget.

New Quest - Classic · Wedding from a Fox howl

Door sign that appeared out of nowhere, a strange wedding host inside a ghostly mansion, what secret may they hold?

Availability: Permanent after the version update.

Eligible conditions: Longchao Pavillion location is unlocked and reach director reputation level "trumpcard 4".

New Dragon Domain - "Ginkgo and Old Mansion"

A hollowed out mansion that's long been abandoned, only a gingko tree left standing beautifully

Unlocking conditions: Unlock the Classic Quest “Crossing Wangchuan - Old Mansion and Cat".

New Dragon Domain - "A Trapped Wedding"

A dragon city maze covered in untamed vines, doesn’t matter if you’re the to be wedded or the guest, don’t think of escaping.

Unlocking conditions: Unlock the Classic Quest “Wedding from a Fox howl- A New Year and those imprisoned".

New Limited Characters (Dragon Descendents)


S Tier Character - Wuchang

Role - DPS;

Element - Water;

Speciality - Anti-Air, Water Element Damage.

Wuchang is a psychopomp with special ability, just the the lore, after Wuchang use elemental skill, she’ll enter a Yin Realm state, in the field created by the state she can fly freely and not take damage. In this condition Wuchang’s normal attack and element skill attack will be enchated, when her said attack hit enemy she also hit them with Soul Lose which will accumulate her Soul Yearn meter, which could be use and consumed to deal bigger water damage.

S Tier Character - Zhu Sha

Role - Support ;

Element - Yin;

Speciality - Normal Attack Buffer, Of-field dps.

As a Fox Demon from the Wu Li District, Zhu Sha is not great with socialising with others. In combat she won’t reveal her true self easily. Instead she’ll summon Festive Caiju* and Xiuqiu* to help her fight. After Zhu Sha use her elemental skill, she'll create 5 Festive Caiju, continuously dealing yin element damage tot he enemy. Her elemental skill will also create a Xiuqiu, which will link to our on field character, boosting their atk based on Zhu Sha atk, after using her ult Xiuqiu will increase the team crit damage and heal the selected character.

Notes from Alkane: *彩鞠 caiju - literal translation colourful ball, it’s a very beautiful medium size ball with thread wrapped around it and intricate design from xinjiang culture.

Watch the visio below (Chinese language):

*绣球xiuqiu - fabric ribbon wrap in a way to resemble flower, think of it as the chinsese versiok of wedding bouquet, funfact hydrangea in chinese is call 绣球花 xiuqiuhua(hua mean flower btw).

Gameplay and QOL changes


New System - Ascention and refinement

After the version 1.2 update, triple(max) refined Amber rank dragon curse could be further refine to Gold rank，and the base stats would increase dramatically. Gold rank dragon curse could refine up to 30 times, each refine would increase the base stats, consume The Seed of Essence* of the corresponding element for refinement.

Notes from Alkane: *new item, but it's basically the as the one you use for weapon refinement for the purple and amber rank but yk, this is one is for the gold rank.


Colour pallette and Dye Plan

Outfit Due - new dye function. Using the cooler colour pallette will allow you to unlocked a dye not own by you. Consume 2 dye bottle allow you to due once. Cooler colour pallette include more rare and beautiful colour！

When the plan for the dye is confimed，button at the bottom right could be used to save the current plan. Update to 3 plan could be saved, switching between plans will not cost dye bottles.

Costume Gifting

A need function will be added toDragon Trend Mall. Director could select costume from theShop to gift it to your friend, along with a custom blessing card and drawing. Receptor could collect gift from the friend account UID.

New gamemode - Dark Tide Crusade

Although the last expedition you assisted Zhen Long Si* in was successful, the enemies in the Dragon City(domain) have been more and more active recently, so Zhen Long Si has issued you a new commission...

The main interface has added a new gamemode entrance for [Dark Tide Crusade]. In this gamemode, the director can launch expedition challenges against three different BOSS every week and participate in the expedition ranking. Detailed rules can be viewed in the corresponding gamemode after the update.

Eligible conditions: Complete the Main Story Quest "Ready Player One" and reach director reputation level "Top Stream 2”.

Notes from Alkane: *The bureaucracy of the game world, Zhai Xi, Yu Ying, Xin Mu and Qin Chuan work here.


Daily Challenge - Dust Illusion Realm(tower/abyss)

Added new challenge forDust Illusion Realm。InDaily Chanllege a new level will be available everyday; after beating the level a prize could be claimed。A maximum of one could be won everyday and up to 3 of it could be stored.

Eligible conditions: Complete all the none time limited floor.

Card Game - Duo di zhu - "Fight the landlord"

Lion District「Flower Market Square」has added a new duo di zhu gamemode; the rule is the same as traditional duo di zhu. Every player will be given 17 cards and each taking turn to fight the landlord, 2 players will act as the peasants to defeat the 1 player acting as the landlord. When a player emptied all their card, the camp they belong to win.

Eligible conditions: Unlock after unlocking Lion District- White Lion street location.

Daily Advancement - * - Dark Tide Crusade

After the version update,Dark Tide Crusade will be a new challenge target, along with Mirage Treasure House(domain)", Arcade", and Sudden Mirage Dream(tower/pvp) will form the four small stages of Daily Advancement. After participating in the Dark Tide Crusade gamemode and reaching enough times, you can obtain the Seed of Essence of random element!
