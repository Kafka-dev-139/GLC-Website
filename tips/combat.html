COMBAT

BY Kafka (layout), Slothlo (information), October 2024

Tutorial - Combat Mechanics

Each character has a basic attack sequence and a skill:

You can accumulate energy by collecting Soul Orbs (Director).

When a character collects enough Soul Orbs or uses their skills, they will be able to unleash an "Ultimate" (not the game's term, for familiarity).


Characters on your team are assigned a key next to their icon (or touch for mobile) by the top right of the screen.

The bar you see below the enemy's health bar is a "Toughness" meter. Attacks will fill that gauge and stagger the enemy when it's full, putting the enemy in a weakened state. The large shield indicator that pops up on the enemy shows the moment it is in the weakened state.

When conditions are met, character icon QTE prompts signals when you can swap the character onto the field, releasing their "Entrance Skill". The Entrance Skill targets the enemy with less health.



Characters roles

Breaker: Can break an enemy's toughness and put them in a weakened state relatively quicker compared to the other two roles.

DPS: Main damage dealer.

Support: Supports the team.
