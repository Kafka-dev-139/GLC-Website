ZHU

Zhu kit and building guide
By Kafka (layout and code), Gema, Chelo, Alkane (translation); October, 2024

Zhu is a 5★ rarity character from the Fire element who's role is DPS.

Attribute - Fire

Height - 181

Birthday - December, 4th

Ocupation - Taoist Priest

Note! There may be localization errors and will be edited when official EN localization is published

GENERAL KIT


Talent

When basic attacks hit, they accumulate “Curse Power.” Upon reaching a certain amount of “Curse Power,” the next attack releases an enhanced blow, dealing high Fire attribute damage.

Basic Attack: Seven Star Sword Stance

Zhu swings the Seven Star Sword, performing up to four consecutive attacks that deal Fire damage and knock enemies forward. Each hit also accumulates a certain amount of “Curse Power.”

Aerial Attack:

Executes up to three consecutive slashes, dealing Fire damage. The final slash performs a downward strike, dealing Fire attribute damage, and each hit accumulates “Curse Power.”

Downward Attack:

While performing an aerial basic attack, pressing the down direction will trigger a dive attack, dealing Fire attribute damage and accumulating “Curse Power.”

Dash Attack:

While dashing, performs a piercing strike, dealing Fire attribute damage and accumulating “Curse Power.”


Skill: Severing Sword Rain

Commands the Seven Star Sword to shoot a rain of swords forward, dealing Fire damage.


Field Skill: Thousand Floating Blades

When an enemy enters a weakened state, dashes forward, piercing through enemies and dealing Fire attribute damage. Along the path, shadow blades fall, dealing multiple instances of Fire attribute damage.


Ultimate: Exorcising Evil

Using an exorcism spell, deals multiple instances of Fire attribute damage to enemies in a large area to the front, while dispelling barriers within the area of effect. After casting this skill, enters the “Curse Power Burst” state, which lasts for a period of time.

Curse Power Burst state increases Fire attribute damage output.


BUILDING GUIDE

- SPECIAL DRAGON SPELL -

General Effect: When dealing NA, it increases Crit DMG by 2.9% for 5 seconds, stacking up to 5 times. Gains additional 10.4% skill DMG on full stacks. Duration resets after repeat.

Special Effect : Increases the numbers of follow-up sword energy strikes of "Severing Evil Flame Slash" by 7 times, each strike's DMG scales with current level.


Skill leveling Priority

Ultimate > Talent > NA > Skill > On-Field Skill


Recommended Relics

QUICK START GUIDE

Skill tips:

- Extra ability 1 "Pure Heart"

Upon casting On-Field Skill "Thousand Floating Blades", the "Curse Power" is immediately raised to MAX. Frequent switch On-Field Skill can refresh Zhu's skill "Severing Evil Flame Slash", creating small burst

- Extra Ability 2 "Avert Disaster"

While in "Curse Power" state, NA can accumulate additional 5 points "Curse Power". The BUFF effect after releasing Ultimate can quickly accumulate curse power to refresh Skill.

- SKILL COMBOS -

Basic combo: Skill > Ultimate > NA > Skill > NA

Combo after breaking enemy's toughness: On-Field Skill > Ultimate > Skill > NA > Skill > NA > Switch character
