YU BING

Yu Bing kit and building guide
By Kafka (layout and code), Gema, Chelo, Alkane (translation); March, 2025

Yu Bing is a 5★ rarity character from the Light element who's role is Support.

Attribute - Light

Height - 165 cm

Birthday - March, 11th

Ocupation - Leader of Yu clan


CV

Jiang Jingju 蒋静菊

Also known for:

Acheron - "Honkai: Star Rail"

Tsukishiro Yanagi - "Zenless Zone Zero"

Raiden Shogun - "Genshin Impact"

Raiden Mei - "Honaki Impact 3rd"

Note! There may be localization errors and will be edited when official EN localization is published

GENERAL KIT


Talent - Eclipse Bird and Moon

Yubing gains an attack power bonus based on her own energy recharge level.

NORMAL ATTACK: Icy Fan Dance

Swings her fan to perform multi-stage continuous attacks, dealing Light elemental damage.


BATTLE SKILL: Imitated Orchid Ink Dot

Swings her fan to release ink droplets, dealing Light elemental damage.

ncreases the attack power of all allies based on her current attack power.


ENTRANCE (FIELD) SKILL: Broadcast Time

Yu Bing appears in the area, dealing AoE Light elemental damage.

Yu Bing increases her own energy recharge rate.


ULTIMATE SKILL: Painting Mountains and Rivers

Yubing opens her fan, releasing floating ink birds to summon Ink-Stained Birds.

Ink-Stained Birds: On hit, deals Light elemental damage and leaves behind Water Ink Orbs.

Water Ink Orbs: Restores energy to the nearest ally upon contact. Up to three orbs can exist simultaneously and persist for 7 seconds, automatically prioritizing allies with the lowest energy.


BUILDING GUIDE

SPECIAL DRAGON SPELL

General Effect Plum Blossom Paint: When casting Ultimate, the character's ATK increases for 10 seconds.

Special Effect Light Ink Plum Blossoms・Plum Blossom Paint: When Yu Bing casts on-field skill, both her and next switch characters' ATK increased for 10 seconds.


SKILL LEVELING PRIORITY

Skill = Ultimate > Talent > On-Field Skill > NA


RECOMMENDED RELICS

Concealing Talent

2 pc: Energy Recharge increased by 18%

4 pc: When using skill, recharge 15% of energy for ultimate, effect could be triggered once every 18 seconds

Main stats:

  • Shoes: Recharge % bonus
  • Bag: ATK % bonus
  • Accessories: ATK % bonus

Sub stats:

ATK % bonus > Recharge % bonus > Extra ATK > Extra DEF


Divine will

2 pc: Yang element damage increased by 12%

4 pc: When dealing damage, ignores 20% of the target's defense

Main stats:

  • Shoes: Recharge % bonus
  • Bag: ATK % bonus
  • Accessories: ATK % bonus

Sub stats:

ATK % bonus > Recharge % bonus > Extra ATK > Extra DEF

QUICK START GUIDE

Skill Tips: When Yubing casts Ultimate "World of Painted Mountains and Rivers " it opens the ancient fan and deploy Ink-Dyed Fleeting Life Realm, continuously summon "Ink-Dyed Flying Birds".

When enemy got hit by "Ink-Dyed Flying Birds", they deal Yang DMG, leave behind "Ink Spheres". "Ink Spheres" helps the character quickly refresh their Ultimate, making them suitable for chara with high Ultimate DMG multipliers.

SKILL COMBOS:

On-field skills > Skill > Ultimate > Switch Character
