WUCHANG

Wuchang kit and building guide
By Kafka (layout and code), Gema, Alkane (translation); January, 2025

Wuchang is a 5★ rarity character from the Water element who's role is DPS.

Attribute - Water

Height - 141 cm

Birthday - July, 15th

Ocupation - Ferryman (direct translation) or psychopomp (localisation)

GENERAL KIT


Talent

Wuchang gains a stacking damage boost to her attacks based on her attack power. Every 100 attack points increase damage by 0.8%, up to a maximum of 40%.

Basic Attack: Tides of the Three Paths

Tap: Wuchang wields her staff and shoots up to three water projectiles toward the front, targeting enemies. Upon hitting, the projectiles deal small-scale Water elemental damage to the surrounding area.

Hold: The normal attack can be charged for greater power. Once fully charged, Wuchang spins her staff and releases a large water projectile that homes in on the enemy, dealing additional Water elemental damage.

Mid-air: When cast mid-air, Wuchang transforms into a water sphere, which crashes into the ground and deals Water elemental damage.


Skill: Return to the Underworld

Activation: When entering the Entering the Dark state, Wuchang’s normal attacks are replaced with Spirit-Resonating Arrows and her special skill changes to Wave of Dissolution.

She gains the Dominance effect, which lasts for 12 seconds.

Dark State

Entering the Dark State: Wuchang expands her domain, dealing instant damage to all enemies within a wide area.

While in this state, Wuchang can freely fly and move within the domain, replacing dash and jump effects with teleportation for quick repositioning.

This state lasts for a maximum of 15 seconds, and the Entering the Dark effect ends automatically if Wuchang leaves the domain area.

Spirit Resonating Arrows

Wuchang fires elemental Spirit Arrows toward the front in a cone-shaped area, dealing Water elemental damage to enemies hit.

Each hit places 3 layers of the Soul Mark effect on enemies, which stacks with the Wave Dissolution buff.

At full stacks (12 layers), Wuchang’s attacks split into two water projectiles that automatically track enemies for continuous damage.

This effect persists for the duration of the state, during which Wuchang’s abilities cannot be modified or interrupted.

Wave of Dissolution

Wuchang unleashes a wave forward, consuming all Soul Marks and dealing massive Water elemental damage within a specified range.

Once this skill is used, Wuchang cannot use the same skill again during the current Entering the Dark state.

The cooldown prevents further ability modifications or transitions.


Field Skill: Surging Wave of the Labyrinth

Wuchang marks the targeted enemy and releases multiple water blades around them. The water blades deal multi-stage area Water elemental damage upon hitting enemies.


Ultimate: Torrent of the Forgotten River

Wuchang summons the waters of the Forgotten River, which cascade down from above onto enemies. This attack deals high Water elemental damage to enemies within the area of effect.

Additionally, Wuchang gains 50 points of Soul Mark energy upon activation. When the ultimate is used during the Entering the Dark state, Wuchang’s actions are immune to interruption.
