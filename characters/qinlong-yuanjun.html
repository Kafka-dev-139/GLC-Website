QINLONG-YUANJIN

Qinlong Yuanjun kit and building guide
By Kafka (layout and code), Gema, Chelo, Alkane (translation); November, 2024

Qinlong-Yuanjun is a 5★ rarity character from the Water element who's role is Breaker.

Attribute - Water

Height - 169 cm

Birthday - Fevruary, 7th

Ocupation - Lionguard leader


CV

Xie Ying 谢莹

GENERAL KIT


Talent

When Azure Dragon is present, it increases the skill effect of “Dragon Surge” and grants a “Resonance” stack, increasing power by 200. If “Resonance” is fully charged, the next “Tide Surge” is empowered, dealing bonus damage.

Normal Attack: Flowing Dragon Tail

Basic Attack: Uses twin swords to perform a multi-stage attack on enemies in front, dealing Water attribute damage.

Charged Attack: Holding the basic attack button on the third stage triggers a charged attack. After charging, launches a powerful forward thrust, followed by continuous spinning slashes that deal multiple stages of Water attribute damage. Each hit accumulates a set amount of “Frost” on the target.


Normal Attack: continued...

Lunge Attack: When released during a lunge, it triggers a piercing-type attack that deals Water attribute damage.

Thrust Attack: When pressing the basic attack button during a dash, rapidly spin the red paper umbrella, dealing two stages of Dark attribute damage.

Flash Counter: After a perfect dodge, releasing a basic attack triggers a powerful pursuit. Vanishes from the original position and reappears near the target, striking downward to deal Water attribute damage.

Aerial Release: Releasing the basic attack in midair performs a downward slash, dealing Water attribute damage.


Skill: Azure Dragon Emerges

Pulls nearby enemies closer, dealing damage, and grants the “Azure Dragon’s Presence” state upon activation.

- Azure Dragon’s Presence -

This state lasts for a limited duration, after which it enters cooldown.

While active, Azure Dragon Emerges upgrades to Rising Tide Wave. The direction of the wave release can be controlled by input.

Hitting an enemy with Rising Tide Wave resets the skill’s cooldown and reactivates the “Azure Dragon’s Presence” state.

In midair, the skill can be enhanced to unleash a stronger version of Rising Tide Wave.


Entrance Skill: Sword Breaks Mountains and Rivers

This skill activates when the enemy enters a weakened state. Performs multiple slashing attacks with twin swords toward the front, dealing damage in several stages. Afterward, a divine soldier is summoned to deliver the final blow.


Ultimate: Submerged Dragon in the Abyss

Summons a Frost Dragon, dealing multiple stages of area damage and pulling enemies to the center.

When the Frost Dragon submerges into the ground, it causes massive area damage and freezes enemies within the area.

Upon release, the entire team gains the “Deep Dive Mastery” state.

- Deep Dive Mastery -

In this state, the critical hit rate increases for a limited duration.



BUILDING GUIDE

- SPECIAL DRAGON SPELL -

General Effect Sword Edge: Increases toughness break efficiency by 21.0% When casting a Skill, increases ATK by 10.2% for 13 seconds.

Special Effect Bewitching Heart.Lost Property: When equipped, it activates "Dragon's Intimidation" state on the enemy.

After breaking Dragon's Intimidation: In this state, the wearer will deal AoE DMG every second equal to wearer's 60% ATK, lasting for 15 seconds. Only 1 enemy can have this state active at a time.


SKILL LEVELING PRIORITY

Ultimate > Skill > Normal Attack = Talent > On-field Skill

QUICK START GUIDE

Skill tips - Extra Ability 1 - "Frost Dragon's Walk", in the same battle, when Yuanjun enters the field for the first time, "Frost Breath" is at full limit. Yuanjun with full "Frost Breath" will enter toughness break burst state.

Pay attention to switch character before entering the battle.

Extra Ability 2 - "Frost Dragon Coil": When Yuanjun isn't on the field, if other characters in the team have dealt Crit hits 10 times, they can trigger a special "Sword Breaking Mountain and River". When activated, Yuanjun herself does not enter the field, summoning Frost Dragon to deal AoE DMG, freeze enemies within the range. After triggering, it enters a CD state for a period of time. When Yuanjun isn't on the field, she can increases other character's DMG, but the characters that on field needs to have a high Crit Rate.
