GENERAL KIT


Talent

After using Skill: Luocha Soul Heart, gain the Demon Umbrella state. Demon Umbrella increases the damage of basic attacks and transforms the final hit into Demon Umbrella Spin, dealing additional damage. Lasts for 5 seconds.

Normal Attack: Red Umbrella Dance

Wields the red paper umbrella to perform up to five consecutive attacks, dealing Dark attribute damage.

When used in midair, performs up to three consecutive attacks, dealing Dark attribute damage.


Basic Attack: Demon Umbrella State

After using Skill: Luocha Soul Heart, enter the Demon Umbrella state. This enhances the range and damage of normal attacks, and the final hit of the basic attack is transformed into Demon Umbrella Spin.

Thrust Attack: When pressing the basic attack button during a dash, rapidly spin the red paper umbrella, dealing two stages of Dark attribute damage.

Flash Dodge Thrust: Within a short period after triggering a dodge, pressing the basic attack button will cause a rapid thrust towards the enemy, dealing two stages of Dark attribute damage.


Skill: Luocha Soul Heart

When cast on the ground, rapidly spins, creating a vortex of air and pulling nearby enemies downward, dealing Dark attribute damage. At the peak of the spin, a large number of souls are released, dealing multiple stages of Dark attribute damage to enemies below.

When cast in mid-air, briefly pauses before rapidly descending to the ground, slamming down and dealing Dark attribute damage. Shortly after landing, souls are released, dealing large-area Dark attribute damage.


Field Skill: Umbrella Descent

When the enemy enters a weakened state, this skill is triggered. The umbrella unfolds to the side, and the user leaps upward diagonally, spinning the umbrella rapidly to attack the enemy, dealing multiple stages of Dark attribute damage.


Ultimate: Ghost Umbrella Night Walk

Unleashes the power of the ghostly umbrella, charging at enemies within a certain range, dealing multiple stages of Dark attribute damage. The character then rises to the sky and gathers energy before descending with a powerful strike, dealing massive Dark attribute damage.



BUILDING GUIDE

- SPECIAL DRAGON SPELL -

General Effect: Lost Property: After casting skill, Crit Rate increases by 6.0% for 8 seconds, can be stacked up to 2 times.

Special Effect: Bewitching Heart.Lost Property: When casting "Demon Umbrella: Cyclone" (Talent), ATK increases by 12.0% for 10 seconds, can be stacked up to 2 times, the duration is refreshed every stacks.
