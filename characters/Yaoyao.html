YAO YAO

Yao Yao kit and building guide
By Kafka (layout and code), Gema, Chelo, Alkane (translation); March, 2025

YaoYao is a 5★ rarity character from the Metal element who's role is DPS.

Attribute - Metal

GENERAL KIT


Talent

When dealing damage, accumulates Love’s Power based on damage dealt.

When Love’s Power reaches its maximum, the next skill Energy Release will be upgraded.

Energy Release Upgrade: Deals enhanced Gold elemental damage to enemies in a larger area.

NORMAL ATTACK: Bucket Chain Hammer

Tap to perform consecutive hammer strikes forward, dealing Gold elemental damage.

Hold to unleash a powerful spinning attack, dealing multi-stage Gold elemental damage.

Can generate more Love’s Power.


BATTLE SKILL: Energy Strike

Lifts a massive hammer, slamming it down on a wide area to deal Gold elemental damage.

Generates Love’s Power.

Upgraded version: Enhanced Energy Strike deals increased damage and affects a larger area.


Field Skill: Broadcast Time

Yaoyao suddenly appears above the enemy’s head, delivering a single devastating hammer blow that deals Gold elemental damage.


Ultimate: Heart-Spinning Hammer

Spins a massive hammer from the sky, dealing multi-stage Gold elemental damage.


BUILDING GUIDE

SPECIAL DRAGON SPELL

General Effect Smile: After dealing DMG with NA or Dragon's Return, the DMG of the next Skill is increased.

Special Effect Wholehearted Response・Smile: Each layer of "Cheer" grants a portion of DMG increase to "Vigorous Release".


SKILL LEVELING PRIORITY

Skill > Ultimate > Talent > NA > On-field Skill

  • Bag: Crit Rate / Crit DMG
  • Accessories: Metal DMG % bonus

Sub stats:

Crit DMG > Crit Rate > Extra ATK > ATK % bonus

QUICK START GUIDE

Skill Tips: Extra Ability 1 determines that Yaoyao needs to start with CA to maximize DMG increase (lasts 10 seconds). Therefore, she needs to use this ability once every 10 seconds. Next, use NA to accumulate "Power of Love".

"Power of Love" is displayed as a progress bar above her HP bar. When the progress bar is full, use her Skill to enhance it into "Vigorous Release" for higher DMG.

Extra Ability 2: "All Out Effort" can make the Skill ATK inflict a Paralysis effect. When Yaoyao casts "Vigorous Release" on a paralyzed enemy, the DMG is increased. Combined with the Paralysis effect of the Metal attributes, it'll maximized the DMG.

SKILL COMBOS:

Ultimate (Accumulating "Power of Love") > CA (Increase the DMG) > NA (Accumulating "Power of Love") > Enhance Skill ("Vigorous Release") > Switch character
