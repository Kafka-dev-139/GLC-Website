YU YING

YU Ying kit and building guide
By Kafka (layout and code), Gema, Chelo, Alkane (translation); November, 2024

Yu Ing is a 5★ rarity character from the Fire element who's role is Support.

Attribute - Fire

Height - 175 cm

Birthday - November, 7th

Ocupation - Purgor


CV

Xiao Qingyuan (Kiyo) 萧清源

Also known for:

Ellen Joe - "Zenless Zone Zero"

Taoqi - "Wuthering Waves"

Note! There may be localization errors and will be edited when official EN localization is published

GENERAL KIT


Talent

When the judgment attack hits the enemy, “Mark of Harvest” debuff will be applied on the target.

Mark of Harvest: units marked will take increased damage.

Basic Attack: Moon Chain Dance

Swings the chain blade to perform up to four consecutive attacks, dealing Fire attribute damage.

While in the air, performs up to three consecutive attacks, dealing Fire attribute damage.


Skill: Judgment Spin

Tap: swings the chain blade and pulls the enemy closer, dealing multiple instances of Fire attribute damage.

Hold: after a brief charge, swings the chain blade and pushes the enemy away, dealing multiple instances of Fire attribute damage.

You can only tap when skill is cast up in the air.


Field Skill: Hunting Moment

When the enemy enters a weakened state, the skill is triggered.

Uses the chain blade to grab the enemy then slashes them, dealing Fire attribute damage.


Ultimate: Forbidden Prelude

Uses the chain blade to bind and restrain the enemies, then plays the solemn prelude of a forbidden melody.

Pulls in enemies within a large area, continuously dealing Fire attribute damage and absorbs HP.

A final explosion deals another instance of powerful Fire attribute damage.


BUILDING GUIDE

SPECIAL DRAGON SPELL

General Effect Greetings: After releasing Ultimate Skill, the character gains 12.8% DMG bonus for 10 seconds.

Special Effect Special Courtesy・Greetings: Enemies under the "Harvest Mark" will have their DEF reduced by 11.0%.


SKILL LEVELING PRIORITY

Talent > Skill = Ultimate > On-Field Skill > Normal-Attack


RECOMMENDED RELICS

Crimson Pansy

2 pc: Increase fire element damage by 12%

4 pc: When using skill, attack is increased by 28%, last for 10 seconds

Main stats:

  • Shoes: ATK % bonus
  • Bag: HP % Bonus
  • Accessories: Fire DMG % bonus

Sub stats:

ATK % bonus > HP % bonus > Extra HP > Extra ATK


Concealing Talent

2 pc: Energy Recharge increased by 18%

4 pc: When using skill, recharge 15% of energy for ultimate, effect could be triggered once every 18 seconds

Main stats:

  • Shoes: ATK % bonus
  • Bag: HP % Bonus
  • Accessories: Fire DMG % bonus

Sub stats:

ATK % bonus > HP % bonus > Extra HP > Extra ATK

QUICK START GUIDE

Skill Tips: - On-Field skill (Hunting Time), Skill (Judgement Spin), and Ultimate (Confinement Prelude) have Crowd Control (CC) effects.

Skill can be used with/without Charge ATK. Both skills have different effects.

The additional skill 2 "Little Games" launches 2 additional fireballs after casting any skill, which can further increase the DMG dealt by Yu Ying.

SKILL COMBOS:

Combo 1: On-Field Skill > Skill > Ultimate > Switch character

Combo 2: Skill > Switch character
