FUYUN

Fuyun kit and building guide
By Kafka (layout and code), Gema, Chelo, Alkane (translation); November, 2024

Fuyun is a 5★ rarity character from the Light element who's role is Support.

Attribute - Light

Height - 180 cm

Birthday - July, 23th

Ocupation - da-shixiong of Wen Shu Lion Dance Troupe


CV

Su Shangqing 西呱双

Note! There may be localization errors and will be edited when official EN localization is published

GENERAL KIT


Talent: Lion’s Awakening

When Lion’s Roar in All Directions is activated, the entire team gains the Guiding Light effect.

Activating Awakened Lion Descends the Mountain or Lion Roars to Calm the World consumes Lion’s Might.

Lion’s Might regenerates automatically by 1 point every second, up to a maximum of 100 points.

Activating Lion’s Roar in All Directions consumes all Lion’s Might and increases the effect of White Lion Armor based on the Lion’s Might used (2% per point).

When Lion’s Might is full, Fuyun will automatically enter “Calm Lion Stance.”

Guiding Light: Increases his own damage reduction, lasting 12 seconds.

Normal Attack: Lion Form Fist

Fuyun performs a powerful series of up to five consecutive strikes on the target, dealing Light-element damage.


Skill: Lion’s Roar in All Directions

Tap: Fuyun raises the lion’s head, dealing Light-element damage to surrounding enemies.

Hold: Fuyun raises the lion’s head and releases a loud roar, dealing heavy area Light-element damage to enemies and activating his White Lion Armor, which boosts his defense, lasting 12 seconds.


Entrance Skill: Awakened Lion Descends the Mountain

When entering the battlefield, Fuyun summons the lion’s soul, causing a shockwave from his position and dealing damage to all surrounding enemies. Nearby enemies also have their movement speed reduced.


Ultimate Skill: Lion Roars to Calm the World

Fuyun channels the lion’s spirit, emitting a powerful shockwave from his position and dealing massive Light-element damage to surrounding enemies. This skill consumes all Lion’s Might, and a single target can only be hit once by each shockwave.


BUILDING GUIDE

SPECIAL DRAGON SPELL

"Rich and Noble World"

General Effect Benevolence: Increases Fuyun's DEF by 8.2%. When casting Skill, this effect further increases Fuyun's DEF by 16.4%, lasting for 6 seconds.

Special Effect BenevolenceOn "Illuminating the Eyes" state, based on Fuyun's DEF, it'll increases Crit DMG of all team. Every 1 point of DEF increases Crit DMG by 0.01%. The a cap limit is 16.8% Crit Damage.


SKILL LEVELING PRIORITY

Talent > Skill > Ultimate > On-field Skill > Normal-Attack

QUICK START GUIDE

Strategy tips - Fu Yun "Lion's Might" has two mechanisms: - When "Lion's Might" is fully charged, it will refresh the Skill. Therefore, Director can cast Skill according to the situation. If "Lion's Might" is about to reach 100, you can tap + hold to cast Skill.

Only by holding down the Skill will consume "Lion's Might". Director needs to grasp the release timing of tapping and holding down the Skill.

SKILL COMBOS:

On-Field Skill > Ultimate > Hold down the Skill > Switch character
