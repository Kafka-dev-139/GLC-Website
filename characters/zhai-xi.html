ZHAI XI

Zhai Xi kit and building guide
By Kafka (layout and code), Gema, Chelo, Alkane (translation); November, 2024

Zhai Xi is a 5★ rarity character from the Dark element who's role is Support.

Attribute - Dark

Height - 178 cm

Birthday - April, 14th

Ocupation - Artificer / Spiritist


CV

Yang Boyu 杨博宇 源

Note! There may be localization errors and will be edited when official EN localization is published

GENERAL KIT


Talent

For each negative status on the enemy target, final damage dealt is increased.

Basic Attack: Resentful Tremor

Summons a ghostly hand to perform up to a three-stage continuous attack, dealing Dark attribute damage.


Skill: Resentful Ghost Assault

Activate the forbidden tome to summon a "Phantom Soul Illusion", dealing Dark attribute damage in an area of effect.

Phantom Soul Illusion

Functions as a summoned creature that follows the character on the battlefield, automatically attacking the closest target and dealing Dark attribute damage.

At regular intervals, it will emit "Resentment" that continuously tracks and attacks enemies, dealing multiple stages of Dark attribute damage.

When the effect duration ends, the illusion will rush towards the nearest enemy, causing a final explosion that deals Dark attribute damage in an area of effect before disappearing.


Field Skill: Eternal Arrival

Triggered when enemies enter a weakened state or when the "Phantom Soul Illusion" disappears. Summons a ghostly hand that pushes forward, dealing damage to enemies hit and knocking them back.


Ultimate: Ghostly Phantom

Summons multiple ghostlyhands to deal Dark attribute damage in an area of effect, pulling all nearby enemies closer. Then deals another round of Dark attribute AoE damage, applying the "Dust Sand Sigil" effect to enemies hit.

Dust Sand Sigil: Enemies afflicted will continuously take Dark attribute damage for 10 seconds. This effect is treated as a negative status.


BUILDING GUIDE

SPECIAL DRAGON SPELL

General Effect Wish: When casting the Return Dragon, skill DMG increases by 29.0% for 12 seconds.

Special Effect Long Night Echo・Wish: When summoned creatures cast "Resentment", the summoned creatures gains 24.0% DMG bonus for 6 seconds. .

Skill = Ultimate > Talent > On-Field Skill > Normal-Attack

QUICK START GUIDE

Skill Tips: - Zhai Xi's Skill "Call for Help" increases the number of summoned creatures. After casting the skill, switch to another character to deal DMG.

Additional abilities 1 "Ghostly Entanglement" pulls all nearby enemies towards you. Due to the pulling effect, switch to another character and use On-Field skill for burst DMG. Be careful not to miss the On-Field skill.

Additional abilities 2 "Twilight's End" summons a ghost hand to push enemies away, dealing DMG and knocking them back. Then switch to another character to provide a safe output environment.

SKILL COMBOS:

Maximize DMG then retreat: On-Field skill > Ultimate > Skill > Switch character

Provide Debuff to allies: On-Field skill > Ultimate > Switch character
