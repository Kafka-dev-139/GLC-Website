RELICS

List of available Relic Sets in Guilongchao!
By Kafka (layout and code), Alkane (translation); October, 2024

Relics sets

Domino

2 pc: increase metal element damage by 12%

4 pc: Increase ultimate damage by 24%. After using ultimate, increase skill damage by 28%, last for 10 seconds

Skilled

2 pc: Increase weakness break by 20%

4 pc: When the toughness bar of the enemy is broken, other characters attack increase by 15%, last for 12 seconds

Crimson Pansy

2 pc: Increase fire element damage by 12%

4 pc: When using skill, attack is increased by 28%, last for 10 seconds

Concealing Talent

2 pc: Energy Recharge increased by 18%

4 pc: When using skill, recharge 15% of energy for ultimate, effect could be triggered once every 18 seconds

Cloud covered moon

2 pc: Water element damage increased by 12%

4 pc: When enemy are in weakened (shield bar is orange colour), attack increased by 15%, crit damage increased by 18%, lasting for 10 seconds

Happiness of cat

2 pc: Increased earth element damage by 12%

4 pc: Damage is increased by 7% whenever the wearer do damage, max stack is 3, lasting for 8 seconds, each trigger refreshes the countdown

Nail outcast

2 pc: Attack increased by 15%

4 pc: Attack increased by 12% when using skill, ultimate, swap-in attack, last for 10 seconds, max 2 stacks, countdown are refreshed every time it triggers

Ink Paint

2 pc: Ying element damage increased by 12%

4 pc: When facing enemy with debuff, increase crit rate by 8%, increase crit damage by 16%

Divine will

2 pc: Yang element damage increased by 12%

4 pc: When dealing damage, ignores 20% of the target's defense

Fluffy Snow

2 pc: Increase defence by 19%

4 pc: Increase shield strength by 20%. After creating shield, those protect by shield have their damage increase by 12%, last for 10 seconds

Aoki

2 pc: Increase healing bonus by 16%

4 pc: When providing healing, the healed target will be immune to damage by 20%, and the damage will be increased by 12%, lasting for 8 seconds

Disclaimer! Relic sets Divine will and Aoki are auto-translated. The big image (that shows relics in a set) will be provided later!
