CHEN XIAO

Chen Xiao kit and building guide
By Kafka (layout and code), Gema, Chelo, Alkane (translation); November, 2024

Chen Xiao is a 5★ rarity character from the Earth element who's role is Breaker.

Attribute - Earth

Height - 183 cm

Birthday - August, 7th

Ocupation - Yong master of Chongyi Lion Dance group


CV

Li Zhuoting 李卓霆

Note! There may be localization errors and will be edited when official EN localization is published

GENERAL KIT


Talent

After using his skill, the next normal attack will be enhanced. This enhancement deals more damage and increases breaking effect.

Basic Attack: Basic Technique

Swing the long staff, delivering up to four consecutive attacks, dealing Earth attribute damage.

In mid-air, swing the long staff, delivering up to three consecutive attacks, dealing Earth attritbure damage.


Skill: Mountain Treading Cane

Swing the long staff to attack the enemy, dealing Earth attribute damage.

Skill can be cast consecutively up to 3 times. If not cast within a short period, it will enter a cooldown.


Field Skill: Lion Shadow Strike

When enemies enter a weakened state, this skill is triggered. Chenxiao quickly swings the long staff, dealing multiple Earth attribute damage hits to nearby enemies. Following that, he swings the long staff fiercely, delivering a final powerful blow to the front target, causing a large amount of Earth attribute damage and launching the target into the air.


Ultimate: Black Lion Charge

Summon the Black Lion, dealing Earth-attribute damage to nearby enemies, and granting the entire team the “Black Lion’s Threat” effect.

Black Lion’s Threat: Increases Normal attack damage for 20 seconds.


BUILDING GUIDE

SPECIAL DRAGON SPELL

General Effect Rumour: Increases toughness break efficiency by 21.0%. When casting skill, increases normal ATK DMG by 4.0% for 6 seconds, stacks up to 3 times. Duration refresh every new stacks.

Special Effect Heart of the Fierce Tiger・Rumour: When the character obtains the "Black Lion's Might" effect, increases Crit Rate by 8.7% and Crit DMG by 17.5%.


SKILL LEVELING PRIORITY

Ultimate > Talent > Skill > On-Field Skill > Normal-Attack

QUICK START GUIDE

Skill Tips: - Chen Xiao's Ultimate "Black Lion's Might" can reduced enemy's DEF in the full Yin state. Combined with Yin's 2nd stage effect, it can increase entire team's DEF, and then switch to another character to maximize the damage.

Chen Xiao's Talent "Bloodthirsty Battle" can strengthen the next NA when casting a skill, combined with the three-time skill casting, it allows for a combo of Skill 1 + NA + Skill 2 + NA + Skill 3 + Normal Attack.

Additional ability 1 "Startling Dust" after releasing the Ultimate, the character gains DMG reduction and invincibility, improving Chen Xiao's survivability and faster breaking of enemy toughness.

SKILL COMBOS:

With Ultimate: On-Field skill > Ultimate > Skill 1 > Normal Attack > Skill 2 > NA > Skill 3 > NA > Switch character

Without Ultimate: On-Field skill > Skill 1 > NA > Skill 2 > NA > Skill 3 > NA > Switch character
